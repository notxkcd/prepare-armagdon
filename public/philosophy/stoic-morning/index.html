The Stoic Morning

October 26, 2023 | stoicism, morning

The morning is not for rushing. It is for preparation.

“At dawn, when you have trouble getting out of bed, tell yourself: I have to go to work — as a human being.” — Marcus Aurelius

We must treat our attention as a scarce resource.
